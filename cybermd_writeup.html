I reached out to the two medical students who were interested in creating a web application to allow medical students to study. There are other alternatives, but they wanted to make the questions free rather than charging users $800+. I was the tasked to create the full stack application with a backend and frontend for both users and admins. The users could see their statistics, watch videos, and study questions.
